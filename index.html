Yoga Poses

  • Cobra Pose: Open the heart and roll the shoulders down to promote flexibility in Cobra Pose.
  • One-Legged King Pigeon Pose II: Open your chest and shoulders and challenge your balance as you move step by step into Eka Pada Rajakapotasana II.
  • Supported Headstand: Meet the king. Standing on your head in proper alignment not only strengthens the whole body but calms the brain.
